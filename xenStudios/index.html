Our Story

Xen Wai began her journey to a healthier body when she became discplined in the practice of yoga as a young adult. Her goal was to share her new lifestyle with as many individuals as she could reach. Ten years ago, Xen studios opened its doors. The studio has held thousands of yoga classes and assisted many new yoga instructors in becoming certified.
